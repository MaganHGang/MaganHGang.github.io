Final Year Project EECS Data Warehouse

Project Description

I created a data warehouse that stores data of the EECS department of Queen Mary. It gave me a chance to demonstrate my SQL skills by creating the database & storing large amount of data about EECS students, courses & modules. I learnt more about how data warehouses work by creating decision support queries that a EECS manager can make in the department. Diffcult project though but had fun. It was aimed for 2nd year students studying DB Systems in order for them to understand them better in the labs.

The images show you all the database design for my 2 data marts and regular database known as an operational database. I wanted to have 2 seperate database designs where one is not actually a data warehouse & the other is a design for the 2 data marts (Modules & courss are the services EECS provides to students) in order to see how students understand how a business manager like the EECS (Electronic Engineering & Computer Science department at Queen Mary) manager can use it to make important descisions for that department.
